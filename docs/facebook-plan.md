# Facebook Page Strategy

## Page Setup

- **Page Name:** [Your Brand Name]
- **Category:** Gaming Video Creator + Education
- **Username:** @[yourbrand]
- **Cover Photo:** Collage of Minecraft Add-on + Rhino 3D renders
- **Profile Picture:** Logo or brand mark

## Monetization Requirements (Facebook In-Stream Ads)

- [ ] 10,000 page followers
- [ ] 600,000 total minutes viewed in last 60 days
- [ ] 5+ active video uploads (or 5 previously live videos)
- [ ] Videos must be at least 1 minute long
- [ ] Meet Facebook Partner Monetization Policies

## Content Strategy

### Post Types & Frequency

| Day | Post Type | Content |
|-----|-----------|---------|
| Mon | Image Post | Minecraft screenshot + tip |
| Tue | Video Share | YouTube video link with teaser text |
| Wed | Tutorial Post | Rhino 3D step carousel (images) |
| Thu | Video Share | YouTube video link with teaser text |
| Fri | Engagement Post | Poll, question, or challenge |
| Sat | Reel / Short Video | Behind-the-scenes or highlight |
| Sun | Community Post | Week recap, milestone celebration |

### Content Formats

1. **Native Video Uploads** — Upload 3+ minute edited videos directly (required for in-stream ads)
2. **Facebook Reels** — 30-90s highlights from YouTube videos
3. **Image Carousels** — Step-by-step Rhino 3D tutorials as slides
4. **Text Posts with Images** — Quick tips, Minecraft screenshots
5. **Live Streams** — Monthly Q&A or live Add-on development
6. **Stories** — Daily quick updates, polls, countdowns

### Engagement Tactics

- Ask questions at the end of every post
- Reply to every comment within 24 hours
- Create a Facebook Group linked to the Page
- Run monthly giveaways (Minecraft Add-on codes)
- Use Facebook Events for live stream announcements

## Ad Strategy (Paid Boost)

### Phase 1: Awareness (Month 1-3) — Budget: $5-10/week
- Boost best-performing video posts
- Target: Gaming + 3D Design interest groups
- Geo: Worldwide (English-speaking)

### Phase 2: Growth (Month 3-6) — Budget: $10-20/week
- Promote lead magnet (free Minecraft resource pack)
- Retarget video viewers
- Lookalike audience from engaged users

### Phase 3: Monetization (Month 6+) — Budget: $20-50/week
- Promote Minecraft Marketplace listings
- Sponsored product videos
- Drive traffic to YouTube for watch hours
