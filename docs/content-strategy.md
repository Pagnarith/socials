# Content Strategy — Master Plan

## Brand Identity

- **Channel Name Suggestion:** "PagnaCreates" or "CraftDesign Studio"
- **Tagline:** "From Blocks to Blueprints — Minecraft Add-ons & 3D Design"
- **Niche:** Creative tech — gaming mods + 3D modeling tutorials

## Content Pillars

### Pillar 1: Minecraft Add-on Showcase (60% of content)
- **Add-on Demo Videos:** Showcase features, gameplay with Add-on active
- **Development Behind-the-Scenes:** How you build Add-ons (JSON, behavior packs)
- **Installation Tutorials:** Step-by-step guides for users
- **Update & Changelog Videos:** New features, bug fixes
- **Comparison Videos:** Before/After with Add-on installed

### Pillar 2: Rhino 3D Tutorials (30% of content)
- **Beginner Series:** "Rhino 3D from Zero" — weekly episodes
- **Speed Modeling:** Timelapse 3D creation videos
- **Tips & Tricks:** Quick workflow improvements
- **Project Walkthroughs:** Full product design start-to-finish
- **Plugin Reviews:** Grasshopper, V-Ray, etc.

### Pillar 3: Community & Behind-the-Scenes (10% of content)
- **Q&A Sessions:** Answer subscriber questions
- **Progress Updates:** Revenue milestones, license goals
- **Collaborations:** Guest creators

## Cross-Platform Strategy

```
YouTube (Long-form) → Extract highlights → TikTok (Short-form)
                    → Reels + Carousels  → Instagram (Visual)
                    → Share link + teaser → Facebook (Engagement)
                    → Notify subscribers → Telegram (Automation)
```
