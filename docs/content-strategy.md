- **Channel Name:** iPricky Pagnarith / Chakriya

## Content Pillars (Updated for 3-Stream Revenue)

### Pillar 1: Minecraft Add-on Showcase (40% of content) — AUDIENCE MAGNET
- **Add-on Demo Videos:** Showcase features, gameplay with Add-on active
- **Development Behind-the-Scenes:** How you build Add-ons (JSON, behavior packs)
- **Installation Tutorials:** Step-by-step guides for users
- **Update & Changelog Videos:** New features, bug fixes
- **Comparison Videos:** Before/After with Add-on installed
- **🎯 Purpose:** FREE content pulls massive audience → funnels to products & donations

### Pillar 2: Rhino 3D Plugins & Tutorials (40% of content) — REVENUE ENGINE
- **Plugin Demo Videos:** "Watch what this plugin does" → link to purchase
- **"I Built This Plugin" Series:** Behind-the-scenes of plugin development
- **Beginner Tutorial Series:** "Rhino 3D from Zero" — weekly episodes
- **Speed Modeling:** Timelapse 3D creation videos
- **Tips & Tricks:** Quick workflow improvements (showcasing plugin features)
- **Project Walkthroughs:** Full product design using your plugins
- **Plugin Reviews:** Compare your tools vs manual workflow
- **🎯 Purpose:** Tutorials build trust → plugin demos convert to sales

### Pillar 3: Community & Behind-the-Scenes (20% of content) — TRUST BUILDER
- **Progress Updates:** Revenue milestones, new plugin launches
- **Q&A Sessions:** Answer subscriber questions
- **"Support Our Work" Posts:** Natural donation asks after delivering value
- **Collaborations:** Guest creators
- **🎯 Purpose:** Builds personal connection → drives donations & loyalty
