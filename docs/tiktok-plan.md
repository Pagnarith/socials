## Video Production Workflow

```
1. Record (screen capture or gameplay)
      ↓
2. Edit in CapCut / DaVinci Resolve
      ↓
3. Add trending audio/music
      ↓
4. Add text overlays & captions
      ↓
5. Create eye-catching thumbnail/cover
      ↓
6. Write caption with hashtags
      ↓
7. Post at optimal time
      ↓
8. Engage with comments (first 30 min critical)
```

## Growth Tactics

1. **Hook in first 1 second** — Most critical for TikTok algorithm
2. **Use trending sounds** — Increases discoverability 3x
3. **Post consistently** — Algorithm rewards daily posting
4. **Engage immediately** — Reply to comments within 30 minutes
5. **Stitch popular videos** — Leverage existing viral content
6. **Cross-promote** — "Full tutorial on my YouTube" in bio
7. **Use TikTok LIVE** — Weekly live sessions boost followers
8. **A/B test hooks** — Try different opening lines same content
